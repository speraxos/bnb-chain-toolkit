# FAQ

#### What is Purity Finance?

Purity Finance converts NFTs, dust tokens and unused accounts into your preferred asset in a single click, helping you reclaim hidden value from your wallet.

#### How does Purity Finance work?

Purity Finance lets you swap and burn up to 30 tokens and NFTs and at a time into a token of your choice. Tokens with liquidity pools will be swapped, while those without will be burned. Burning a token reclaims the SOL used for storage, which we then swap into your chosen token.&#x20;

Additionally, Purity Finance can close up to 50 vacant accounts (accounts with a zero balance) at a time, earning approximately 0.002 SOL per closed account. This process helps you recover SOL from unused accounts, further maximizing your gains.

#### Can I burn NFTs?

Yes, you can burn NFTs on our homepage. You can burn up to 30 at once, or combine NFTs and tokens for a total of 30 assets per transaction. Regular NFTs yield about 0.01 SOL when burned, while Core NFTs yield about 0.001 SOL. When you burn a cNFT, you don't get SOL back. This is because all cNFTs in a collection share a single account, so burning individual cNFTs doesn't close an onchain account that would return SOL.

#### What kinds of NFTs can Purity Finance burn?

We currently support burning regular NFTs, Core NFTs, and Compressed NFTs (cNFTS).

#### What are Purity Points?

Earn Purity Points with each wallet Purification. The more you Purify, the more points you will earn. Purify daily to unlock a powerful streak multiplier.

#### Who made this?

We are a small Pure team of friends, artists, and developers with a combined experience of 10 years in the crypto space.

#### Are there fees?

The fee for burning a token or closing an empty account is 0.0004 SOL per action. For burning NFTs, the fee is 0.0006 SOL for regular NFTs, 0.0004 SOL for Core NFTs, and 0.0001 SOL for cNFTs. Token swaps carry a fee of either 1.5% of the transaction value or 0.001 SOL, whichever is higher. These fees support Purity Finance's infrastructure, API costs, and continued development.

#### What chains are supported?

Currently we only support Solana, but we plan to support EVM chains in the future.

# Pure Wallet FAQ

#### What is Pure Wallet?

Pure Wallet is an automated wallet Purification tool that earns for you automatically. It's ideal for degens and copytraders, taking our manual Purification service a step further by securely holding your wallet on a backend and performing regular, scheduled Purifications.

#### How does Pure Wallet work?

Pure Wallet runs on a schedule and automatically swaps tokens that are below your set value threshold to your target token. It can also close vacant accounts. To prevent unintended swaps, we strongly recommend adding important tokens, like LP tokens, to the "Tokens to Ignore" list before running Pure Wallet.

#### Why does Pure Wallet require my wallet seed phrase or private key?

We require your private key to automate transactions, similar to how Telegram trading bots like Photon or Trojan operate. To ensure your private key's security, we use AES-256 encryption to store it on our backend. The decryption key is accessible only on our production backend and cannot be accessed by our team.

#### What are Purity Points?

Earn Purity Points with each wallet Purification. The more you Purify, the more points you will earn. Purify daily to unlock a powerful streak multiplier.

#### Who made this?

The Purest souls in crypto.

#### Are there fees?

For each empty account closed, the fee is 0.00035 SOL. For token swaps, the fee is either 1.2% of the transaction value or 0.0008 SOL, whichever is greater. These fees go towards infrastructure and API expenses, and support further development of Purity Finance.

#### What chains are supported?

Currently we only support Solana, but we plan to support EVM chains in the future.
